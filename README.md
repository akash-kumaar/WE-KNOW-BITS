# AMRx: Smarter Antibiotic Awareness

Antibiotics are life-saving tools, but their misuse is fueling a global crisis. AMRx is an AI-driven platform designed to help you understand when these medications are truly necessary and the long-term risks of using them incorrectly.

---

## The Global Reality

Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is no longer a future threat; it is a current emergency. According to the latest data from The Lancet and the World Health Organization (WHO):

* **Current Impact:** As of 2021, bacterial AMR was directly responsible for 1.14 million deaths annually and played a role in an additional 4.71 million deaths.
* **The 2050 Forecast:** Experts project that between 2025 and 2050, more than 39 million people will die directly from antibiotic-resistant infections.
* **Rising Risk for Seniors:** While deaths in children under five have declined, deaths among adults aged 70 and older have increased by over 80% since 1990 and are expected to more than double by mid-century.
* **Regional Burden:** The crisis disproportionately affects South Asia and sub-Saharan Africa, though resistance is rising globally due to the over-prescription of "last-resort" drugs like carbapenems.

## The Mission

Overusing or improperly taking antibiotics leads to resistance, making standard treatments less effective for everyone. We built AMRx to turn complex medical data into clear insights, helping people avoid unnecessary prescriptions and understand the impact on their future health.

## How it Works

By analyzing symptoms and prescriptions, our analytical AI system—focused on diagnosis, drug safety, and resistance prediction—provides explainable guidance rather than just a simple "yes" or "no" answer.
